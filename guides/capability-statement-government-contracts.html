What is a Capability Statement?

A Capability Statement is a concise business document used to explain what your company does, who you serve, what makes you qualified, and how government buyers or prime contractors can contact you.

What should it include?

  • Company overview and core services
  • Differentiators and strengths
  • NAICS codes and relevant classifications
  • Past performance or comparable experience
  • Certifications and socioeconomic status, if applicable
  • Contact information and company identifiers

Why it matters

A Capability Statement gives buyers a quick way to understand your business. It can support outreach, teaming conversations, vendor registrations, and opportunity review.
