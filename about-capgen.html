About CapGen

CapGen is a government contracting intelligence concept from Apropos Group LLC. It is being developed to help small businesses turn a business capability profile into a more focused government contract opportunity pipeline.

What CapGen helps organize

Capability profile

Clarify what the business does, who it serves, and what work it can perform.

NAICS direction

Connect services to industry codes that matter in government opportunity research.

Opportunity review

Review opportunities that may match the business profile, service category, and pursuit readiness.

Bid/no-bid thinking

Consider whether an opportunity is worth pursuing before spending time on a response.

Public positioning

CapGen helps small businesses identify government opportunities that may match their capabilities, NAICS codes, business profile, and pursuit readiness.

What CapGen is not

  • CapGen is not a government agency.
  • CapGen does not guarantee contract awards.
  • CapGen does not guarantee certification approval or eligibility.
  • CapGen does not replace legal, compliance, procurement, or proposal advice.
