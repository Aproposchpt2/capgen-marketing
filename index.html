Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Is CapGen a government agency?

No. CapGen is a private business tool from Apropos Group LLC. It is designed to help businesses organize and review government opportunity information.

Does CapGen guarantee government contracts?

No. CapGen does not guarantee awards, eligibility, certification approval, or contract success. It helps identify opportunities that may match your business profile and supports better pursuit planning.

What is a NAICS code?

NAICS stands for North American Industry Classification System. Government buyers use NAICS codes to classify the type of work or service in an opportunity. CapGen uses your registered NAICS codes to match relevant opportunities from the federal marketplace.

Who should access CapGen?

Small businesses, veteran-owned businesses, SDVOSB firms, service providers, consultants, IT companies, call centers, administrative support firms, and vendors actively pursuing government contracts.

Is CapGen free to access?

Free access is available for businesses that join the CapGen beta program. Beta access includes the full platform with no feature restrictions.
