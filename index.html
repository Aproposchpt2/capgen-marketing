Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for business owners reviewing CapGen early access.

Is CapGen a government agency?

No. CapGen is a private business tool from Apropos Group LLC. It is designed to help businesses organize and review government opportunity information.

Does CapGen guarantee government contracts?

No. CapGen does not guarantee awards, eligibility, certification approval, or contract success. It helps identify opportunities that may match your business profile and supports better pursuit planning.

What is a NAICS code?

NAICS stands for North American Industry Classification System. Government buyers use NAICS codes to classify the type of work or service in an opportunity.

Who should request early access?

Small businesses, veteran-owned businesses, SDVOSB firms, service providers, consultants, IT companies, call centers, administrative support firms, and vendors exploring government contracting.
