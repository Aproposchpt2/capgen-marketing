Privacy Notice

This privacy notice explains how Apropos Group LLC uses information submitted through the CapGen early access form.

Information collected

The early access form may collect your name, business name, email, phone number, website, city, state, primary service, SAM.gov registration status, NAICS status, certification type, and the type of government opportunities you are looking for.

How the information is used

Information submitted through the form is used to contact you about CapGen early access, review whether your business may be a fit for a match preview, and improve CapGen marketing and outreach.

No government affiliation

CapGen is a private business tool from Apropos Group LLC. CapGen is not a government agency and does not represent any federal, state, or local agency.

No guarantee

CapGen does not guarantee contract awards, bid success, eligibility, certification approval, or government opportunities. CapGen helps identify opportunities that may match business information provided.

Contact

Questions about this notice may be directed to Apropos Group LLC through the contact information used in the CapGen early access process.
